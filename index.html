THE STORY

In a world where ancient civilizations never fell, Atlantis rises from the depths not as a myth, but as the last bastion of humanity against an encroaching darkness that threatens to erase both past and future.

The Atlantean follows the journey of the last guardian, awakened from a millennia of slumber to unite fractured realms and prevent the collapse of the timeline itself. Armed with technology beyond comprehension and wisdom older than recorded history, the guardian must navigate political intrigue, ancient curses, and the weight of impossible choices.
